How do we process???


1.Registration

At first the donor has to register himself,by giving necessary details about his name,phone,and other contact details.The donor also need to give information about the book which he is donating like book name,class.Thereafter,a confirmation mail will be sent to the donor.

2.Collection

Once the registration process is successful,our assigned people will come at your doorstep to collect the books.Once we receive the books,we will again send a confirmation mail regarding the books that we received from you.

3.Donation

On receiving the books successfully,we are now ready to donate the books to needy and financially unstable children.Hence,now we will contact the responsible NGO's for this precious purpose.

Our Vision

A world where books are available to all , without the barriers of location or cost. A world where to read a book trees do not have to be cut.

Our Mission

A world where every printed book has been read by five people before it is recycled.
